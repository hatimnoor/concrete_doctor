Polyurethane (PU) flooring is the gold standard for environments that demand seamless, hygienic, and highly durable surfaces. Widely used in food processing, pharmaceutical, medical, and commercial facilities — PU flooring combines flexibility, chemical resistance, and ease of cleaning in one system.

01

PU Coving Floor

A seamless floor-to-wall transition system that eliminates the dirt-trapping joint between floor and wall — essential for hygiene-critical environments.

  • Eliminates bacterial harborage points
  • Standard cove height: 100mm
  • Integral with the floor system — no joins
  • Required by health code in food facilities
  • Available in multiple colors

02

PU Screed Flooring

A heavy-duty polyurethane screed system that provides a seamless, chemical-resistant, and thermally stable surface for demanding commercial and industrial environments.

  • Suitable for wet and dry processing areas
  • Thermal shock resistant (steam cleaning compatible)
  • Thicknesses from 4mm to 9mm
  • Anti-slip aggregate broadcast available
  • Fast-cure formulations for minimal downtime
